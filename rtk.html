Road to Kingdom: Ace of Ace

Road to kingdom: Ace of Ace, is season 2 of "Road to kingdom" from 2020. Ace of Ace a 2024 reality survival show that was broadcasted on Mnet from September 19 to November 7, 2024. The groups that competed was 8turn (2023-present), Younite (2022-present), Oneus (2019-present), The new six(2022-present) , Cravity (2019-present), The crewone (2024-present) and of course Tempest (2022-present). The MC of the show was the legendary idol Taemin, a soloist and member of Shinee (2008-present). There was 8 episodes, releasing each thursday. Although Tempest was the second team elimiated, they performed greatly.

Winner: Cravity

Tempest group performances: RTK

Introduction

In this stage, Tempest performed their song "Lighthouse", with only 1 prop. They ranked second place.

VS stage

This stage was a in the VS round, where Tempest went against The New Six, both covering famous group Stray Kids songs. Tempest covered "Maniac", while TNX covered Phobia. Although TNX won this round and Tempest ranked 5th place overrall. Their cover of Maniac was loved by many, including fans of Stray Kids.

Identity

In the Identity round, Tempest performed a mash up of their songs "Eye of the storm" and "Cant stop shining". The performance had a Alice in Wonderland theme, and was joyful and bright. They ranked 6th place in group performance, but 4th place in Ace performance.

No limit

This was Tempest last group performance in RTK. Tempest performed a cover of SuperM "Jopping" with a futuristic feeling, the watcher being inside of a virtual reality. In the middle of the performance when member Hyuk was being brought up in the air by a wire while singing, the wire was dropped when he was high up, way before it was supposed to. He nearly fell ontop of back up dancers and could of been injured, aswell as the dancers. Hyuk went on with the performance with only just a small hiccup while singing when he fell. There has been said that this was on purpose by the nettwork wanting them to get eliminated, as there was never no problem with the wire in the practices of the stage, and Tempest apperantly having been treated unfairy, and views from their Jopping performance being taken.
