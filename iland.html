I-Land

I-Land is a South Korean reality competition series for boy groups, produced by Mnet and organized by Belift Lab, a joint venture between CJ ENM and HYBE at the time of airing. The show featured 23 male trainees, with seven ultimately selected to debut as Enhypen in the finale on September 18, 2020. The winning members were Jungwon, Jay, Jake, Ni-ki, Heeseung, Sunghoon, and Sunoo. The first six members were chosen based on global rankings, while the seventh member was selected by the producers.

Ngô Ngọc Hưng "Hanbin"

Hanbin the viatnamese member of Tempest was a contestant on I-Land. He was ranked 4th place in episode 10 by global votes, although he was eliminated by the producers in episode 11.
