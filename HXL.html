HyeongseopXEuiwoong

Hyeongseop X Euiwoong is a South Korean duo created by Yuehua Entertainment, consisting of Ahn Hyeongseop and Lee Euiwoong, who both previously competed in Produce 101 Season 2. They made their debut on November 2, 2017, with the single "It Will Be Good." Both members later re-debuted in Yuehua's boy group Tempest on March 2, 2022.

Discography

The Moment of Memory

HyeongseopXEuiwoong debut album released in 2017. Consists of the songs "It will be good" and "I like U" both songs also having a instrumental version.

Color of Dream

Album released in 2018. Consisting of 5 songs. The songs are "Spring Rain" "Love tint", "Love Letter", "Lover Clock", and "Remember".
